Biography

Don Norman is a pioneer in Human–Computer Interaction. He introduced Norman’s Action Cycle and the Gulfs of Execution and Evaluation, helping designers understand how users form goals, execute actions, and interpret system feedback.

Why Norman Matters

Norman’s model explains user errors and usability problems by focusing on cognition and interaction, rather than only interface mechanics.
